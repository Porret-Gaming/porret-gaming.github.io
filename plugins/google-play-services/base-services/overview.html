Google Play Base Services V2 Overview

The Google Play Base Services Version 2

Google Play Game Services include many amazing features for you to make use of to improve your game. Below you can find a list of services provided by Google Play Games with the extra services sold seperately.

  • Google Sign-In
  • Play Games Sign-In
  • Achievements
  • Leaderboards
  • Saved Games [Sold Seperately]
  • Events [Sold Seperately]
  • Admob Advertising [Sold Seperately]

These features solve many problems encountered during mobile Android game development for you.

The Unreal Engine Plugin

Introducing the Unreal Engine plugin for the Google Play Games service, a powerful tool that brings the features of Play Games Version 2 directly to your Unreal Engine projects. This plugin is designed to expose the Games default service's functionality to blueprint scripting, simplifying the integration process for game developers.

With an intuitive and user-friendly interface seamlessly integrated into the Unreal Engine editor, connecting to the service becomes a breeze. Whether you're a seasoned Unreal Engine developer or new to the platform, our plugin empowers you to easily perform all the default features provided by the Play Games V2 Library, enhancing the capabilities of your game.

When making use of this plugin, you must not use the built in Play Games blueprint nodes provided in Unreal Engine. All functionality has been replaced and enhanced by this plugin.

This plugin is compatible with all our other plugins, specifically our Advanced Admob Advertising Plugin and our extended Google Play Games services (Saved Games and Events).
